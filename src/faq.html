Frequently Asked Questions

What degrees can I calculate with this calculator?

Currently we cover Undergraduate Degrees that include one or two years of study. Undergraduate Degrees with Honours (Dissertation) are also included.

Any boring legal stuff I should know about this website?

Yes actually. We're not being paid or serviced by the University to create this website, this is being created in our free time as a self project. We maintain this website ourselves and thus at any point we could seize production. Our website is not affiliated with University of Stirling in any way, and is not representative of their views or values. This calculator is also not a 100% accurate fool proof estimate of your grades, and so you should use this as a guide and not a rule. We don't take any responsibility for your classification/grades being inaccurate to what you receive.

Does this website use grade information for anything other than calculating degrees/module results?

Absolutely not. Although we plan to implement functionality to allow you to store your grade info for later use, this is only being used for your benefit and nothing else. We would never sell on other peoples data. All of our source code is public and you're free to check this yourself.

How accurate is this calculator at calculating my scores/degree classification?

We try to be as accurate as possible to the specification which is laid out by the University, but we of course can't totally predict the algorithm, and the rules could change over time. You should take this as a guide, and redirect any serious grade questions to your Chief Examiner or Personal Tutor.

What about dual programs or more complex degree structures?

Unfortunately, we're unable to reliably cover all of these as they would have many exceptions to the normal calculation rules. You should still be able to attempt to calculate your classification, but it is not as reliable as a normal program.
